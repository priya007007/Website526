Game Description
A 2D, single-player, side-scrolling action-adventure game. Taking place in a world overrun by fear. The main character is Tommy Trojan, a traveler who yearns for a normal world where everyone can travel without worries. In order to achieve this, Tommy goes on a quest to overcome his fears and face the final boss in order to free the world once again. The main character, Tommy Trojan, is inspired by the symbol of USC. Since Tommy is a symbol of strength and bravery to us as USC students, we believe that he serves as a great protagonist for the storyline of this game. In addition, the various settings/stages take inspiration from some of the places on our travel bucket list as well as some of our own fears.
The player can be controlled with the help of a mouse, can move and jump.In the main menu, the game tutorials is there whil will guide users about the game. Each level has different obstacles the character has to go through such as: moving platforms, pits that are lethal.
